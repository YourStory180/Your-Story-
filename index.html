The entire process typically takes 4-5 weeks from start to finish. You'll spend 1-2 hours sharing your story with our AI, then we transform it into your novel and memoir versions. You'll receive drafts for review throughout, and once approved, your books are printed and shipped within 1-2 weeks.

The memoir version presents your life story in traditional autobiography format - straightforward, chronological, and factual. The novel version transforms the same experiences into an engaging story with your chosen genre elements (romance, thriller, mystery, etc.) while maintaining the truth of your experiences. Both honor your authentic story but appeal to different reading preferences.

Our AI acts like a thoughtful interviewer, asking questions about different periods of your life, important relationships, challenges you've overcome, and moments that shaped you. You can respond by speaking (voice recording) or typing. The AI asks natural follow-up questions to help you remember details and ensures we capture the full richness of your experiences.

Absolutely! You can transform your life story into any genre: romance, thriller, mystery, fantasy, literary fiction, or family saga. Each genre version maintains the core truth of your experiences while adapting the storytelling style to match your preferred reading experience.
