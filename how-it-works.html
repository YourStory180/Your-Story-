Detailed Process

Life Story Capture Methods

We offer multiple ways to capture your memories, making it comfortable and convenient for everyone:

  • Voice recordings: Natural conversations guided by our AI prompts
  • Written forms: Structured questionnaires you can complete at your own pace
  • Photo uploads: Share images that trigger memories and stories
  • Family sessions: Include multiple family members in the storytelling process
  • Interview style: Professional interview sessions if preferred

AI Transformation Process

Our AI technology creates two distinct book formats from your stories:

  • Memoir format: Factual, chronological account of your life experiences
  • Novel format: Narrative storytelling with character development and plot structure
  • Voice preservation: Maintains your unique speaking style and personality
  • Professional editing: Grammar, flow, and structure refinement
  • Family tree integration: Character generation for important people in your life

Delivery & Formats

Your completed books come in multiple professional formats:

  • Hardcover books: Premium A4 size with dust jacket and professional binding
  • Digital versions: PDF and eBook formats for easy sharing
  • Custom cover design: Personalized artwork reflecting your story
  • Professional layout: Typography, margins, and formatting optimized for readability
  • Fast delivery: Domestic shipping included, expedited options available
